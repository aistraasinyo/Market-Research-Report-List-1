<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PVC RFID Wristband Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-Frequency (LF)</li><li>High-Frequency (HF)</li><li>Ultrahigh-Frequency (UHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC RFID wristband market comprises three primary types based on frequency: Low-Frequency (LF), High-Frequency (HF), and Ultrahigh-Frequency (UHF). LF RFID typically operates at 125 kHz, suited for applications like access control and animal tracking. HF RFID, operating at 13.56 MHz, is used for smart cards and payment systems. UHF RFID, ranging from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, offers longer read distances and faster read rates, ideal for inventory management and event ticketing, enhancing their versatility across industries.</p></p>
