<p><p>The semi-graphitic cathode block market consists of two key types: bottom blocks and side blocks. Bottom blocks are primarily used in the aluminum reduction process, providing essential thermal and electrical conductivity at the furnace base. In contrast, side blocks are positioned on the sides of the furnace, playing a crucial role in maintaining structural integrity and enhancing heat retention. Both block types are essential for optimizing energy efficiency and performance in electrochemical processes, particularly in aluminum production.</p></p>
