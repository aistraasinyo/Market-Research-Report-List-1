<p><ul><li>Convex Safety Mirror</li><li>Flat Safety Mirror</li><li>Dome Safety Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety mirrors market consists of three main types. Convex safety mirrors offer a wide field of vision and are commonly used in corners and driveways to eliminate blind spots. Flat safety mirrors provide a true reflection, making them suitable for applications where accuracy is vital, such as in retail. Dome safety mirrors combine the features of both convex and flat mirrors, providing a 360-degree view and are often used in large spaces like warehouses for enhanced surveillance and safety.</p></p>
