<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Cameras</li><li>Decentralized IP Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IP CCTV camera market comprises two main types: centralized and decentralized IP cameras. Centralized IP cameras rely on a central server for video storage and management, facilitating easier control and maintenance but requiring a robust network infrastructure. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras process and store footage locally on the camera itself or an adjacent device, offering greater flexibility and reduced bandwidth use. Each type caters to different security needs and operational contexts, influencing deployment strategies and cost considerations.</p></p>
