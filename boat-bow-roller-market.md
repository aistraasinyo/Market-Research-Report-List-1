<p><p>The boat bow roller market primarily consists of two types: standard rollers and pivoting rollers. Standard rollers are fixed and designed to securely hold the anchor in place while allowing for smooth deployment and retrieval. In contrast, pivoting rollers can tilt or move, accommodating various anchor shapes and sizes, which enhances versatility and ease of use. Both types serve to improve anchoring efficiency and protect the boat's bow, catering to different preferences and boating conditions.</p></p>
