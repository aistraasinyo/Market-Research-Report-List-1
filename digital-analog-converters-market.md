<p><strong>The Digital-analog Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>R-2R DAC</li><li>Thermometer-Coded DAC</li><li>Pulse-width Modulator DAC</li><li>Oversampling DAC</li><li>Binary-weighted DAC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital-analog converters (DAC) market comprises several types, each with unique characteristics. R-2R DACs use a resistor ladder for precise output, while thermometer-coded DACs offer high accuracy through binary-weighted thermometers. Pulse-width modulator DACs convert digital signals into varying pulse widths for efficient analog output. Oversampling DACs enhance performance by sampling at higher rates to reduce noise, and binary-weighted DACs utilize a simple binary system to achieve output, catering to different applications in audio, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
