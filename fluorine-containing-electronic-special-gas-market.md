<p><p>The fluorine-containing electronic special gas market includes various gases used in electronic manufacturing and semiconductor processes. Key types include F2 (fluorine) for etching and cleaning, CHF3 (hydrofluorocarbon) for low global warming potential applications, and SF6 (sulfur hexafluoride) for insulation and plasma etching. NF3 is used for cleaning chemical vapor deposition chambers, while COF2 serves in photoresist applications. Other gases like ClF3, CF4, C2F6, C3F8, C4F8, and C4F6 play roles in etching and deposition processes, contributing to advanced manufacturing technologies.</p></p>
