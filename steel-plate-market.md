<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Steel Plate</li><li>Cold Rolled Steel Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel plate market includes **Hot Rolled Steel Plate** and **Cold Rolled Steel Plate**. Hot rolled plates are produced at high temperatures, making them cost-effective and suitable for structural applications, but with a rougher surface. Cold rolled plates are processed further at room temperature, resulting in smoother surfaces, tighter tolerances, and higher strength, ideal for precision applications like automotive or appliances. Both types cater to diverse industries, with hot rolled being more economical and cold rolled offering enhanced finish and durability.</p></p>
