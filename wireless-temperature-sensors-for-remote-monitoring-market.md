<p><ul><li>NTC Thermistors</li><li>Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs)</li><li>Thermocouples</li><li>Semiconductor-Based Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless temperature sensors for remote monitoring include various types designed for different applications. NTC thermistors provide high sensitivity and accuracy at a low cost, making them suitable for consumer electronics. Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) offer excellent stability and precision, often used in industrial settings. Thermocouples are versatile and can operate over a wide temperature range, ideal for extreme environments. Semiconductor-based sensors are compact and power-efficient, frequently used in HVAC systems and medical devices for real-time monitoring.</p></p>
