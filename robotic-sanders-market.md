<p><p>The robotic sanders market consists of various types, with orbital sanders and belt sanders being the most prominent. Orbital sanders utilize a circular sanding pad that moves in random or elliptical patterns, ideal for achieving a smooth finish on flat surfaces. Belt sanders feature a continuous loop of sandpaper, offering rapid material removal for rough surfaces and large areas. The “others” category includes specialized sanding machines, such as spindle or detail sanders, catering to niche applications in woodworking and manufacturing.</p></p>
