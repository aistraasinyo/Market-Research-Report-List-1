<p><p>The cheese concentrate market is primarily categorized into two types: powder and paste. Cheese powder is produced by dehydrating cheese, resulting in a versatile ingredient used in snacks, seasonings, and ready-to-eat meals. It offers convenience and extended shelf life. Cheese paste, on the other hand, is a semi-solid form that retains moisture, making it ideal for spreading, filling, and cooking applications. Both types serve distinct culinary purposes, enhancing flavors and providing functional benefits across various food products.</p></p>
