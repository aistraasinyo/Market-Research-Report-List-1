<p><ul><li>External Blower</li><li>Internal Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Downdraft range hoods are designed to effectively eliminate smoke, steam, and odors from cooking areas. They come in two main types: external blower and internal blower. External blower models feature a motor located outside the kitchen, providing quieter operation and increased airflow capacity, ideal for large cooking environments. In contrast, internal blower hoods have the motor integrated within the unit, offering a compact design suitable for smaller spaces but typically with lower airflow. Each type caters to different kitchen needs and preferences.</p></p>
