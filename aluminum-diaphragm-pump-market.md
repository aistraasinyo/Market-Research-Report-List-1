<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum diaphragm pump market features two main types: single acting and double acting. Single acting pumps use one diaphragm to create pressure in one direction during each cycle, making them efficient for low-flow applications. In contrast, double acting pumps utilize two diaphragms to generate pressure in both directions simultaneously, allowing for higher flow rates and continuous operation. Both types are valued in various industries for their reliability, efficiency, and ability to handle corrosive fluids.</p></p>
