<p><ul><li>Base coat wax</li><li>Top coat wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surfboard wax market is divided into two main types: base coat wax and top coat wax. Base coat wax is applied first to create a solid foundation and improve grip. It is typically softer and designed to adhere well to the board. Top coat wax, applied over the base coat, is harder and offers enhanced traction, ideal for various water conditions. Together, they help surfers maintain control and performance while riding waves.</p></p>
