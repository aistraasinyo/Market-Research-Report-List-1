<p><p>The audio codec market is divided into three main types: mono, stereo, and multi-channel codecs. Mono codecs handle single-channel audio, suited for applications like voice calls and podcasts. Stereo codecs support two-channel audio, commonly used in music and video, enhancing the listening experience. Multi-channel codecs manage multiple audio channels, providing immersive sound for home theaters and gaming. Each type caters to different user needs, influencing market demand and product development in the audio technology landscape.</p></p>
