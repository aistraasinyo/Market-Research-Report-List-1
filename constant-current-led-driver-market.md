<p><strong>The Constant Current LED Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Boost Type</li><li>Buck Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Constant Current LED Driver Market is categorized mainly into Boost Type and Buck Type drivers. Boost Type drivers step up the input voltage to deliver a stable current, making them ideal for high-power applications and scenarios where the input voltage is lower than the LED forward voltage. Conversely, Buck Type drivers lower the input voltage while maintaining a constant current, suitable for applications where the supply voltage is higher, ensuring efficient power management and optimal LED performance.</p></p>
