<p><p>The blow molding tools market consists of three main types: extrusion, injection, and stretch blow molding. Extrusion blow molding involves forming a tube of molten plastic, which is then inflated into a mold. Injection blow molding uses an injection-molded preform that is expanded in a separate mold, ensuring precise shapes and finishes. Stretch blow molding stretches the preform during the inflation process, enhancing clarity and strength, making it ideal for producing bottles and containers with superior quality and durability.</p></p>
